What are operators in Vim (Normal mode)?	Commands that act on a range defined by a following motion (e.g., `dw`, `yip`).
What does the change operator `c` do (Vim)?	Deletes the text covered by a motion, then enters Insert mode.
Operator `>` does what (Vim)?	Indents the text covered by a motion to the right.
Operator `<` does what (Vim)?	Indents the text covered by a motion to the left.
Operator `=` does what (Vim)?	Auto-indents the text covered by a motion.
Operator `!` does what (Vim)?	Filters the text covered by a motion through an external command.
