What does the delete operator `d` do?	Deletes the text covered by a following motion (e.g., `dw`, `d$`).
What does `d2_` do (Vim)?	Deletes from cursor through the first non-blank of the next line (because `2_` moves there).
What does the yank operator `y` do?	Copies (yanks) the text covered by a following motion (e.g., `yw`, `y$`).
