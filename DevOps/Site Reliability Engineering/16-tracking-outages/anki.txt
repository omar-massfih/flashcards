Why is outage tracking necessary in addition to postmortems?	Postmortems cover deep learning for major incidents, while outage tracking reveals frequency, toil, trends, and smaller recurring issues across services.
What is the role of Escalator in Google's alerting workflow?	It tracks whether a human has acknowledged an alert and escalates to the next destination if acknowledgment does not occur in time.
Why is flexible tagging valuable in outage tracking?	It lets teams add lightweight metadata such as causes, actions, customers, or bogus alerts without waiting for a rigid global taxonomy.
How does historical outage data help during incident response?	It helps responders ask what happened last time, compare patterns, and reuse prior diagnoses or mitigations.
How can outage tracking improve shift handoff and periodic reviews?	It can generate compact reports that include subjects, tags, and important annotations so engineers quickly see recent lowlights and open concerns.
