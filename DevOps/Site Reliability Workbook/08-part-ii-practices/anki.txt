What is the core idea of SRE practices?	Apply software engineering solutions to operational problems.
How do SRE practices turn operational problems into engineering work?	They automate, redesign, or algorithmically manage work that would otherwise require manual operations.
Why should SRE teams periodically review their work balance?	Tracking work mix over time helps identify unhealthy operational load, underload, or lack of engineering capacity.
What is operational underload?	A state where engineers spend so little time operating a service that they lose practical knowledge of it.
How can teams counter operational underload?	Take more operationally useful risks, move faster, run disaster recovery tests, onboard related services, or hand back services that no longer need SRE support.
How should operational work feed project work?	Operational pain, toil, incidents, and requests should identify strategic projects that improve production systematically.
What kinds of SRE projects can reduce operational load?	More robust storage, less brittle frameworks, safer rollouts, sharding, automation, and fixes for systemic outage causes.
