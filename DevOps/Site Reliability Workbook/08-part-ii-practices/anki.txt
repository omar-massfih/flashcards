Why should SRE teams periodically review their work balance?	Tracking work mix over time helps identify unhealthy operational load, underload, or lack of engineering capacity.
How should operational work feed project work?	Operational pain, toil, incidents, and requests should identify strategic projects that improve production systematically.
What kinds of SRE projects can reduce operational load?	More robust storage, less brittle frameworks, safer rollouts, sharding, automation, and fixes for systemic outage causes.
